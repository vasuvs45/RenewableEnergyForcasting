Belgium's Inclination towards Solar Energy Adoption

Belgium was ranked as the second country in the world for solar energy capacity per capita, after Australia. The Belgian government has implemented several policies and incentives to encourage the adoption of solar energy including feed-in tariffs, tax incentives, and net metering.These policies have helped to make solar power more affordable and accessible for individuals and businesses.Belgium has also made significant progress in developing large-scale solar energy projects, with several solar farms and solar power plants operating across the country. These projects have helped to increase the country's solar capacity and reduce its reliance on fossil fuels.
